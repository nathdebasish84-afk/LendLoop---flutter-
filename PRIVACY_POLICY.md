# Privacy Policy for LendLoop

**Last Updated: June 7, 2026**

LendLoop ("the App") is designed with privacy in mind. This policy explains how your data is handled.

## 1. Data Collection
LendLoop is an offline-first application. All data you enter, including person names, item descriptions, and transaction amounts, is stored **locally on your device** in a private database.

## 2. Data Usage
The data you provide is used solely for the purpose of helping you track your lent and borrowed items. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
LendLoop does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## 4. Data Backup & Export
You have full control over your data. You can export your data to a CSV file at any time. This file is saved to your device's storage and is under your control.

## 5. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions:
- **Notifications:** To provide reminders for due dates.
- **Storage/Files:** To save and share exported CSV reports.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@lendloop.app.
